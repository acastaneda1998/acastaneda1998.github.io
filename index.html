The Sport Known As Golf

A very interesting topic to me is that of the sport of Golf. Unlike any other sport, anyone can play it no matter how old or how young, yet it is still very difficult to be good at. The sport likes to reward those who have a better mentality than those who excel with more physical stregnth as smarter thinking tends to have the upper hand over longer distances.
